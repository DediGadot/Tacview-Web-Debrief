Weapon Effectiveness Analysis

Weapon Analysis Statistics Guide
Weapon Usage Chart
  • X-Axis: Weapon types used in mission
  • Y-Axis: Total shots fired per weapon
  • Bar Height: Indicates weapon popularity/usage
  • Color: Light blue for all weapons
Effectiveness Scatter Plot
  • X-Axis: Total shots fired
  • Y-Axis: Effectiveness percentage (kills/shots)
  • Bubble Size: Number of kills achieved
  • Position: Top-right = most effective weapons
Effectiveness Calculation
  • Formula: (Kills ÷ Shots) × 100
  • High %: Weapon is very lethal
  • Low %: Weapon requires many shots per kill
  • Zero %: Weapon fired but no kills recorded
Tactical Insights
  • Bubble Size: Larger = more total kills
  • Weapon Labels: Positioned above data points
  • Comparison: Usage vs. effectiveness analysis
  • Strategy: Identify most/least effective weapons
